BIO

WHO AM I?

I'm Hamza Iqbal, a Radar Systems Engineer with a passion for solving complex technical challenges. My background in mathematics and experience in signal processing has led me to work on innovative radar technologies for the automotive industry.

PROFESSIONAL JOURNEY

After completing my BSc in Mathematics at the University of Missouri-Columbia, I worked as a Data Engineer at Washington University in St. Louis. There, I managed data structures and performed time-series analysis for healthcare research.

In 2022, I joined Uhnder Inc., an automotive radar and chip design startup, where I've been developing tools for analyzing radar signal processing pipelines, creating visualization systems for sensor coverage, and testing prototype modules.

Recently, I completed a Post Graduate Program in AI & ML at the University of Texas-Austin, enhancing my skills in machine learning algorithms and AI applications.
